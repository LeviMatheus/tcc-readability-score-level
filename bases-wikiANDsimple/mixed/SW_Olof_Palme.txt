He was the leader of the Social Democratic Party from 1969-1986 to his death and Prime Minister of Sweden from 1969 - 1976 and 1982 - 1986. He was shot dead in Stockholm after going to the cinema on February 28 1986. The killer has never been arrested, and the gun has never been found.
