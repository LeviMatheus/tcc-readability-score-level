Albert Dean Rosellini (born January 21, 1910) is a lawyer from the U.S. state of Washington. Rosellini is part of the Democratic Party and was the Governor of Washington (1957â€“1965) for two terms. He is the oldest living person to have been a governor of a U.S. state, and was the first Italian American Catholic to become a governor of a state west of the Mississippi River. He was called "Taxellini" by people who did not think he was doing a good job as a governor, while he was called "The Gov." by people who did think he was doing a good job.
