Robert Kubica (born 7 December 1984 in Krakow) is a Polish racing driver.
Kubica obtained four victories in 17 races and won with 154 points clinching the title from Adrian Valles and Markus Anglesquats. On 1 December 2005, he got his first Formula 1 test for the Renault F1 Team to drive their R25. Subsequently, he was given a drive for the BMW Sauber F1 team as test and reserve driver for the 2006 F1 season.
