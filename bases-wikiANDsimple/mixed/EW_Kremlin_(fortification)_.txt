
A kremlin or kreml (, , "castle", or "citadel") is a major fortified central complex found in historic Russian cities. This word is often used to refer to the most famous, the Moscow Kremlin, or metonymically to the government that is based there. The word is of Russian origin which itself might originate from Tatar language. The word may share the same root as "kremen" (, , "flint"). Some kremlins in Russia are called "detinets", as for example the Novgorod Detinets.
