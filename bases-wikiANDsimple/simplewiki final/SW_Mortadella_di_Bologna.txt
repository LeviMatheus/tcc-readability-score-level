Mortadella di Bologna is an Italian kind of sausage. It is made of ground pork meat. Salt, pepper, sugar as well as large pieces of bacon are added. It is then made into big sausages. It is then cooked in special ovens at 90Â°C. Some kinds of Mortadella have other spices in them.
As a Protected designation of Origin, Mortadella di Bologna must come from the Bologna province.
