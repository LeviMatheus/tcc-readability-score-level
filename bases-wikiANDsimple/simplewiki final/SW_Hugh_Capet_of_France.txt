Capet was the first person of the direct Capetian Dynasty to rule France.Hugh would make the office of the king hereditary and would pass his crown down to his son.The dynasty he establish would rule France unbroken for 300 years.
"Capet" is a nickname for "wearing a cape".
Hugh Capet is buried in the Saint Denis Basilica.
