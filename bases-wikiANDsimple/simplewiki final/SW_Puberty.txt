Puberty is the name for a process that turns children into adult humans. Puberty is started by hormones. Girls go through puberty from about age 10 to age 18, boys from about age 12 to age 20. Changes that happen to a teenager's body during puberty include the onset of menstruation, the penis enlarging, breasts growing, hair growing around the genitals, under the arms, on the face and on the legs. Some people get hair on their chest or their back. Other things that happen during puberty is the growth of the vocal chords. This means that the voice will get deeper.
