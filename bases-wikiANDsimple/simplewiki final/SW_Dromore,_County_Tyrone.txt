Dromore ("Droim MÃ³r", meaning "Big Ridge", in Irish) is a village in County Tyrone, Northern Ireland. It is situated nine miles (15 km) south west of Omagh on the A32 road and sixteen miles (26 km) from Enniskillen. The village had a population of 1,101 in 2001. Agriculture and the building trades are the primary sources of employment in the town. It is situated in the Omagh District Council area.
