Phetchabun () is one of the northern provinces ("changwat") of Thailand. As of 2000, 965,784 people live there, and has an area of 12,668.4kmÂ². The Governor is Direk Thuengfang.
Administrative divisions.
The province is subdivided in 11 districts ("amphoe"). These are further subdivided into 117 subdistricts ("tambon") and 1261 villages ("muban").
