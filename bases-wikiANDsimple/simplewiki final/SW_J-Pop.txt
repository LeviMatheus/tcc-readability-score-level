J-Pop is the popular music of Japan. Recently, J-Pop has been influenced (affected) by western culture, J-Pop now sounds different than older J-Pop.
Yumi Matsutoya.
Yumi Matsutoya, also known as Yuming, Yumi Arai and Karuho Kureta, is considered by many the "mother" of modern-day J-Pop. She has been one of the most successful Japanese singer-songwriters in the twentith century in the world of pop music.
